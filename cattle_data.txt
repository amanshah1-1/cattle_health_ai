Our product is a cattle health monitoring system designed to improve livestock productivity and early disease detection.

The system continuously monitors cattle behavior using sensors. It tracks key parameters such as rumination, activity levels, resting patterns, and movement.

Rumination monitoring helps detect digestion issues and stress in cattle. A decrease in rumination can indicate potential health problems.

Activity tracking helps identify abnormal behavior such as reduced movement, lameness, or illness. Sudden spikes or drops in activity can signal health risks.

The system generates different types of alerts:
- Health Alerts: Triggered when abnormal patterns are detected.
- Inactivity Alerts: Generated when cattle show very low movement.
- Attention Needed Alerts: Indicates possible early-stage disease or unusual behavior.

Farmers receive real-time notifications through a mobile application. This helps them take quick action and prevent serious health issues.

Early disease detection reduces treatment costs, minimizes losses, and improves overall farm productivity.

The system also stores historical data, allowing farmers to analyze trends and make better decisions.

This solution helps in improving animal welfare, increasing milk production, and optimizing farm operations.
